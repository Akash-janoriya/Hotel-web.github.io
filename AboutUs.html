Hotel Narmda River View

No matter how many safaris you've been on, there is nothing quite as wonderful and unforgettable as having breakfast or tea in front of Maa Narmada River in peaceful place at Narmada River View..

Know More »

Nearest

A 9-minute walk from the nearest metro station, this modern hotel in the city centre is 3 km from the Albert Hall Museum and 4 km from the 18th-century Hawa Mahal palace.

Rooms

Contemporary rooms, many with wood floors and floor-to-ceiling windows, feature flat-screen TVs, free Wi-Fi and safes, plus minibars, and tea and coffeemaking facilities. Room service is available.

Parking

Parking is complimentary. There's also a rooftop grill restaurant, a bar and a cafe, as well as a gym and meeting/event space. Breakfast is available for a surcharge.
